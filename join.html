Open positions

We are a new lab and are actively looking for curious, motivated people who enjoy working at the interface of physics, computation, and biology.

What we look for

We value curiosity, rigor, and a willingness to engage with hard problems across disciplinary boundaries. Prior experience in physics, biophysics, computational biology, or a related quantitative field is a plus, but enthusiasm and a strong foundation in quantitative reasoning matter most.

Our work is primarily theoretical and computational — a comfort with math and programming (Python, C++, or similar) is helpful. We collaborate closely with experimental groups and expect all lab members to engage with the biological context of our work.
